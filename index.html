About the Candidate

Belmont is where I chose to raise my family — and I show up for it every day. My kids are in Belmont Public Schools. I've led the PTA, spent years getting neighbors together, and nearly two decades at Deloitte working across strategy, operations, and some genuinely complicated budget decisions. I want to be useful to the place I call home — Town Meeting feels like exactly the right way to do that.
